Welcome to Satyen Bose Academy

Located in the heart of Joykrishnapur, we are breaking the stereotype of private education. We don't believe in rote learning or burdening children with heavy bags.

Instead, we focus on Holistic Development. Our mission is to build a strong foundation in English so our students can compete globally, while ensuring they remain deeply connected to their local culture and the Bengali language.

  • Based in Joykrishnapur (Bishnupur, Bankura)
  • Safe and nurturing environment
  • Affordable quality education

Our Academic Structure

Designed for the developmental needs of every age group.

Nursery

Age: 3+ Years

Focus on social skills, motor skills, and learning through play and music.

LKG

Age: 4+ Years

Introduction to alphabet, numbers, drawing, and basic phonics.

UKG

Age: 5+ Years

Sentence formation, basic logic, creative arts, and storytelling.

Class 1 - 4

Age: 6+ Years

Core subjects, English fluency, Bengali literature, and critical thinking.

Why We Are Different

English + Bengali

We are an English-focused school, but we give enough emphasis on Bengali. We want our students to be global citizens who are proud of their roots.

No Pressure, Just Learning

Contrary to popular belief about private schools, we don't put immense pressure on kids. Childhood is precious, and learning happens best when the mind is free from fear.
